Cheesecake

A delicious cheesecake to sweeten any occasion

Ingredients

Crust

  • graham crackers crushed
  • 28g confectioners' sugar
  • 74g butter, melted
  • 1/8 teaspoon table salt
Filling
  • 452g cream cheese, room temp.
  • 152g granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
